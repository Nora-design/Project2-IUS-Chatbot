Sarajevo, October 31, 2013

Pursuant to Article 76 of the Law on Higher Education (“Official Gazette of Canton Sarajevo”,
issue No. 43/08, 18/10, and 15/13) and Article 4 of the Statute of the International University of
Sarajevo, Senate of the International University of Sarajevo, on its session held on October 31,
2013, adopted the following

DECISION

I
It is hereby established the right of IUS graduates to receive diploma and diploma supplement at
prearranged time of the month upon their written requests and after all financial obligations towards
IUS are fulfilled.

Il
Relevant IUS offices are instructed to issue diploma and diploma supplement to the IUS graduate
upon the verification of the fact that conditions for issuing the documents are met by the graduate
and not exceeding 30 days from the day IUS received the request.

Ill
In case a failure to fulfill any of the obligations prescribed by a curriculum, or failure to fulfill all
financial obligations is found on the part of the graduate, relevant office will provide written
document to the graduate in which it would reject his/her request until all requirements are
completed. ‘

IV
This decision enters into force upon its adoption.
