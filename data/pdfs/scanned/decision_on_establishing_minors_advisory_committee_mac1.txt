DECISION

I
The Minors Advisory Committee (MAC) on the study programs of the organizational units of the
International University in Sarajevo is being formed. MAC consists of the following members:

1. Program coordinator of the major study program;
2. Program coordinator of the minor study program;
3. Faculty secretary.
Il
The MAC has the following tasks:

1. To review the student's request;

2. To determine whether the student meets the requirements for enrollment to minor degree in
regards to students’ achieved CGPA and ECTS credits;

3. To advise the student and, if necessary, request additional documentation from the student;

4. Based on all the above, make a recommendation to the dean of the student’s major program.

The faculty secretary is obliged to review the formal and legal orderliness of the request, carry out the
minor degree procedure and submit the final decision to the Student & Career Center (SCC), the Student
Affairs Office (SAO) and the university archive.
