- BOARD OFTRUSTEES -

Number: FRO-20/09-2021/139
Date: September 20", 2021

Pursuant to the Article 9 of the Rulebook on Awarding Scholarships to the Students of First Cycle of Studies
at the International University in Sarajevo, no. FRO-11/02-2020/10 as of February 11th 2020 and item VII of
the DECISION on scholarship criteria for second study cycle no. FRO-11/02-2020/10-1 as dated February
11th 2020, the President of the Board of the Foundation for the Development of Education, brings the
following determination

DECISION

on cancelation of awarded scholarship in case of transfer to another study program

Article 1
In case of transfer to another study program, it is determined that the IUS scholarship student in full loses
the right on awarded scholarship.

Article 2
The scholarship student referred to in Article 1 means a student of the | or Il cycle of studies who enjoys the
right to discount on any basis, but not limited to the basis of: testing, mutual cooperation agreement, OSYM
test, qualification scholarships, humanitarian scholarships, scholarships for prominent athletes, artists and
gifted students, scholarships based on the enrolled brother/sister (of student), scholarships for family
members of employees, scholarships for children of IUS employees, student-demonstrators, scholarships
based on success in first cycle of studies, etc.

Article 3

The decision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption.
