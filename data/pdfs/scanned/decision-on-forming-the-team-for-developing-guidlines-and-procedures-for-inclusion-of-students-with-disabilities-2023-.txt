On forming the Team on developing of guidelines and procedures
for inclusion of students with disabilities

To the purpose of fostering inclusive and supportive environment for all students of the
International University of Sarajevo, the Team on development of guidelines and procedures for
inclusion of the students with disabilities (hereinafter: The Team) is hereby formed, in the following
composition:

1. Assoc. Prof. Dr. Almasa Mulali¢, Lecturer of inclusive education, chair,
2. Assist. Prof. Dr. Ozge Biiyiikdagli, SDG Committee representative,

3. Assist. Muhemmed Huzeyfe Kticiikaytekin, FLW representative,

4. Assistant Faruk Obuéa, Psychology representative,

5. Sabiha Pepié-Inal, SAO Manager,

6. Selma Ibreki¢, Student Inclusion Officer.

I
The Team from the item | is tasked to develop a clear guidelines and procedures for inclusion of the
students with disabilities starting from the enrollment and during the whole studies at the
International University of Sarajevo.
The Guidelines has aim to provide a structured approach for administrative units, academic staff,
and all involved in this process, along with fostering an inclusive and supportive environment at the
International University of Sarajevo.
The Team will collaborate closely with relevant stakeholders for guideline development and
awareness initiatives.
