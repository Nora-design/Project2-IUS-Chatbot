Pursuant to the Articles 17 and 18 of the Rules on Accreditation of Higher Education
Institutions and Study Programs (Official Gazette of Sarajevo Canton No. 21/19), Rector of
the International University of Sarajevo (IUS) adopts the following

DECISION
On Adoption of the Action Plan

The Action Plan for Upgrading and Improvement of the Quality System at the International
University of Sarajevo is hereby adopted and it forms an integral part this Decision.

I
IUS Quality Assurance Office is responsible to publish the Action Plan on IUS website, to
produce annual report on activities undertaken to implement the Action Plan, and to submit
it electronically to the Ministry of Education, Science and Youth of the Sarajevo Canton.

Ml
The decision shall enter into force on the day of its adoption.
