Recognizing the importance of the United Nations Agenda 2030 on Sustainable Development
Goals (SDG) as a guide for the well-being of all people, respecting all legal and strategic
measures adopted in BiH in the field of sustainable development, and taking into account the
implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals at the International University in
Sarajevo (IUS)*, and in accordance with the mission and vision of the University, the Rector of
IUS adopts

SDG POLICY

The SDG Policy at IUS expresses the commitment of the IUS management to continually raise
awareness, build capacity and undertake concrete measures that contribute to the realisation
of the Sustainable Development Goals.

Policy is to provide a clearly defined framework and a systemic approach for the most efficient
and successful implementation of the SDGs at the University.

The framework for the implementation of the Policy (activities, persons in charge, deadlines,
etc.) will be established through the IUS Strategy for the period 2022-2026.

Aware of the role that higher education institutions can play in contributing to sustainable
development, the strategic plans will, among other things, focus on quality education, research
and artistic work, and cooperation with the local and international community.

Concrete measures may include: introduction of thematic units or courses on sustainable
development in curricula; support for student initiatives and their engagement in the field of
SDG; encouraging the active participation of academic and administrative staff in designing and
implementing projects; creation of open educational resources (OER); establishment of
strategic partnerships with institutions, networks and platforms; creation of targeted
promotional content, implementation of SDG awareness campaigns and other socially
responsible and environmental actions; participation in workshops, seminars and projects on
the topic of SDG; and the implementation of the SDG through normative-legal acts of the
University.
