Based on the Article 61, paragraph (1) and in connection with Article 106, paragraph (5) of the Law on
Higher Education (Official Gazette of Sarajevo Canton, number 36/22), the Senate of the International
University in Sarajevo, at its 179th session held on August 1st, 2024, unanimously adopts the following

DECISION
on determining extended deadlines for enrollment of foreign students

|
For the purpose of facilitating the acquisition of the necessary language competencies (as a unique
prerequisite for all courses at the University), as well as assisting with the timely acquisition of visas
and residence permits, this Decision allows for special deadlines for the enrollment of foreign
students, provided that the following conditions are met:

1. The candidates/students in question, within the given (late) deadline and for the relevant
semester, have the possibility of attending classes by the official start date of the English
Language Course (ELS),

2. The candidates meet the formal legal requirements of the announcement,

They have signed a study contract, and
4. There is a enough available spots for enrollment in the given program in accordance with the
quotas from the announcement.
